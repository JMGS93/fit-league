Fit League Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/23/2025

1. Introduction

At Fit League, we are committed to protecting your privacy and keeping your personal data safe. This Privacy Policy explains in detail how we collect, use, store, and protect the information you provide through our app. By using our services, you agree to the terms and conditions described here.

2. Information We Collect

To provide a personalized experience and optimize our app’s performance, we collect the following data:

  • Email Address: Used for account identification and communications.
  • Total Calories: Numeric record of total calories tracked.
  • Caloric Deficit: Numeric value of the user’s configured deficit.
  • Total Days: Number of days tracked, stored as a string.
  • Gender: User’s specified gender.
  • Body Fat: Current and initial body fat percentage.
  • Fat Mass: Numeric value of the user’s fat mass.
  • Muscle Mass: Numeric record of the user’s muscle mass.
  • Target Weight to Lose: Numeric value of the weight goal.
  • Calculated Result: Text result, e.g. “Your body fat is 25.10%.”
  • Saved Meals: Custom meal entries created and stored by the user.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize and improve your app experience.
  • To provide and maintain app functionality.
  • To generate and display personalized results.
  • To communicate updates, service changes, and relevant notifications.
  • To comply with legal and security obligations.

4. Storage and Security

We store your data securely on Google Cloud Firestore. We’ve implemented strong technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Access is restricted to authenticated users only.

5. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or competent authorities.
  • With service providers who support our app under confidentiality agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, limit processing, port your data, or withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at fl_team@hotmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be posted here, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly. Please review periodically.

8. International Transfers

If we transfer your data outside the EEA, we’ll use appropriate safeguards such as EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

9. Data Retention

We keep your personal data only as long as necessary for its intended purpose or as legally required, then securely delete or anonymize it.

10. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a breach affecting your personal data, we will notify affected users promptly and detail the mitigation measures. To report a breach, email fl_team@hotmail.com.

11. Advertising & Third Parties

We use third-party ad services (e.g. Google Mobile Ads) that may collect non-identifiable device and usage data to optimize ad relevance. These data are used solely to improve ad targeting and managed under the providers’ privacy policies. You can manage ad personalization in your device settings.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at fl_team@hotmail.com.

13. Billing Data

To process subscriptions we collect transaction ID, purchase/renewal dates, and the email linked to your Google Play or App Store account. These are stored securely and used only to verify subscription status, handle renewals, and process refund requests.

14. Renewals & Cancellations

Subscriptions auto-renew at the end of each period (monthly or yearly). You can cancel auto-renewal in Google Play or App Store settings—the cancellation remains effective until the end of the paid period.
